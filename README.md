# Blackjack

A fully playable Blackjack game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

🎮 **Play here:** https://zhipengwu714.github.io/BlackJack/

---

## Features

- Full Blackjack game logic — hit, stand, bust detection, and dealer auto-play
- Double down — double your bet for exactly one more card
- Blackjack pays 3:2 — natural Blackjack gives a 1.5x bonus payout
- Hidden dealer card — reveals when you stand
- Chip betting system with a slider to set your bet
- Win/loss tracking saved between sessions
- Player name saved across sessions
- Leaderboard tracking your best and current chip count
- Out of chips detection with reset prompt

---

## How to Play

1. Enter your name and click **Set Name**
2. Use the slider to choose your bet amount and click **Place Bet**
3. Click **Start Game** to deal
4. Choose **Hit** to draw a card, **Stand** to end your turn, or **Double Down** to double your bet for one final card
5. The dealer reveals their card and draws until they hit 17 or more
6. Closest to 21 without going over wins!
